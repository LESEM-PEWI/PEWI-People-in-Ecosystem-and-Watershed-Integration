Press the "Esc" key on your keyboard to access the Instructor Options menu. Here, you can find a number of features in PEWI to customize as you see fit. Each landuse type can be toggled on and off, hover options can be configured, hotkeys can be adjusted, years available can be changed , precipitation can be turned off, and application statistics can change. To save your changes, press "Save and Exit" Once you return, you can see your changes made.
